Boys & Girls Clubs of Cleveland provides safe, fun places for kids to go after school. At our Clubs, caring adult mentors help kids reach their full potential by focusing on academic success, healthy lifestyles and good character, as well as by offering arts and career readiness programs. Shoes and Clothes for Kids needs your help to ensure that lack of appropriate school uniforms, shoes and supplies aren’t barriers to getting an education for more than 11,000 local school aged children living in poverty each year. The first community foundation in the U.S. and one of the largest in the world, it gives out more than $100 million in grants every year to support efforts in education, youth development and the arts. Each and every donation helps to provide free programming and transportation to hundreds of children with physical disabilities and their teen volunteers.
